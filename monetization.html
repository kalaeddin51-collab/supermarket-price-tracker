Revenue Strategies

Affiliate / Referral Links

Easy

Add tracked referral links to Woolworths, Coles, and Harris Farm product pages. Earn 1–3% on orders placed via click-through. Works today with zero friction.

$200–$800 / mo at 5 k MAU

Freemium — Pro Tier

Medium

Free: 1 shopping list, 3 watchlist items, weekly price updates.
Pro ($4.99/mo): unlimited lists & watchlist, daily scrapes, price history charts, CSV export.

$500–$5 k / mo at 2–5% conversion

Display Advertising

Easy

Google AdSense or Carbon Ads in sidebar / below basket totals. Low CPM (~$3–6 AU) but completely passive once set up. Hide ads for Pro users.

$50–$300 / mo at 10 k MAU

Data Licensing

Hard

Aggregate anonymised basket-price time-series and sell to FMCG brands, market researchers, or financial analysts tracking CPI proxies. Requires legal review.

$2 k–$20 k / year per client

White-Label / API

Hard

Expose a REST API for price lookups and basket comparisons. Target loyalty apps, meal-kit services, and personal finance tools. Charge per request or flat monthly.

$500–$5 k / mo per B2B client

Sponsored Store Features

Medium

Sponsored "Specials" badge or featured store placement negotiated directly with IGA, Harris Farm, or Aldi AU. Performance-based CPM or flat sponsorship.

$500–$3 k / mo per sponsor

Unit Economics Summary

Affiliate Revenue per MAU

Click rate × AOV × commission = revenue per active user. At 8% CTR, $85 AOV, 2% commission: $0.136 / MAU / mo. Scales linearly.

Pro LTV

Avg. churn for tools like this: ~15%/mo → avg. lifetime 6–7 months → LTV at $4.99 = ~$30–$35 / subscriber. CAC should be < $10.

Break-even (hosting only)

At $45/mo hosting (Railway Hobby): need 330 MAU at base affiliate assumptions, or just 9 Pro subscribers. Very low bar.

$1 k/mo Revenue Target

Affiliate only: ~7,400 MAU. Pro subs only: ~201 subs. Mixed (60/40): ~4,000 MAU at 2% Pro conv. Achievable within 12 months with SEO + referrals.
